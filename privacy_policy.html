Privacy Policy

for BeoBus • Last updated: 7 June 2026

BeoBus ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This app is designed to show live public bus locations in Ireland and collects zero personal data.

We collect nothing. Zero. Nada. Zilch.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.
  • We do not collect device location or any usage data.
  • IP addresses may be temporarily processed by our servers solely for rate limiting and security. They are not stored or used for any other purpose.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, cookies, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not create user accounts or require login.
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties.

What the app does

The app displays live bus locations and timetables by fetching publicly available real-time data from the National Transport Authority’s open APIs (https://developer.nationaltransport.ie/). It also uses your device’s location to show your position on the map. All location processing happens locally on your device only — your location data never leaves your phone and is never sent to our servers or any third party. We collect and store nothing.

Children’s privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your region).
